Our Exciting Rafting Trips

Beginner's Delight

Perfect for first-timers and families, our Beginner's Delight trip offers a gentle introduction to the thrills of rafting. You'll navigate through calm waters and small rapids, surrounded by breathtaking scenery. Our expert guides ensure a safe and enjoyable experience for all.

  • Difficulty: Easy
  • Duration: 3 hours
  • Min. Age: 8 years

Adventurer's Challenge

Step up the excitement with our Adventurer's Challenge trip. This intermediate-level journey features more challenging rapids and longer stretches of whitewater. You'll put your skills to the test while enjoying the rush of adrenaline and stunning natural landscapes.

  • Difficulty: Intermediate
  • Duration: 5 hours
  • Min. Age: 12 years

Extreme Explorer

For seasoned rafters seeking the ultimate thrill, our Extreme Explorer trip delivers heart-pounding excitement. Navigate through intense rapids, steep drops, and technical maneuvers. This challenging adventure is perfect for those who crave an adrenaline rush and want to push their limits.

  • Difficulty: Expert
  • Duration: Full day
  • Min. Age: 16 years
