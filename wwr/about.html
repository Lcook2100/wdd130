Rapid Riders

Company Purpose: To make people happy while having an adventure.

Mission: To provide a safe and fun rafting experience for everyone.

Creed: We believe that everyone should have the opportunity to experience the thrill of rafting.

Motto: "Ride the Rapids!".

History

We started as a family of adventure lovers. We wanted to share our love of adventure with others, but couldn't find a place that was both fun and family friendly. So we started by saving up money to buy a rafting site. Eventually we were able to buy on in Colorado. After that, we started hiring tour guides, they come from all over! More recently we started offering different levels of difficulty, this allows us to have a range of clients from family friendly to thrill seekers. Everyone can enjoy something at Rapid Riders!
